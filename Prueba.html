Behind Our Structure

ABOUT ABS REPRESENTATIVE

ABS is not a service — it is a structure built on real market experience. Behind every decision lies operational knowledge, commercial judgment, and direct presence at origin.

Historical Perspective

Origin of ABS Representative

ABS was founded in Rosario, the core of the Argentine agri-industrial hub, to address a fundamental imbalance in international trade. For decades, the market has been dominated by "seller-side" logic, where buyers often negotiate from a distance with limited visibility of local operational realities.

ABS was structured to bridge the gap between global procurement requirements and Argentine supply execution, connecting buyer expectations with real operational conditions at origin.

Mission

Why ABS Exists

To restore control at origin.

Most international buyers operate in Argentina without direct control over supplier interaction and execution conditions, creating structural misalignment between decisions and outcomes.

To operate as a local commercial office.

ABS functions as an embedded presence at origin, ensuring that sourcing decisions are executed with real market visibility, operational validation and full alignment with buyer objectives.

Methodology

Operational Approach

01. VALIDATION

We don't just find suppliers; we validate their execution capacity, financial standing, and technical compliance before any commitment is made.

02. VISIBILITY

We provide real-time market conditions in Argentina, allowing buyers to structure operations based on facts rather than speculative offers.

03. REPRESENTATION

We act under mandate, providing a physical and technical presence during negotiations, quality assessments, and logistical coordination.
