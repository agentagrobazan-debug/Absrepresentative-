Context

Most sourcing challenges in Argentina are not caused by supply — but by lack of local control.

Why Companies Establish a Presence in Argentina

Lack of Local Presence

Companies sourcing from Argentina often operate remotely, without direct visibility over suppliers or execution conditions at origin.

This lack of on-the-ground presence creates a structural gap between commercial decisions and actual market reality.

Supplier Risk Exposure

Purchasing decisions are frequently based on incomplete or unverified supplier information.

This leads to misaligned expectations, execution failures and avoidable commercial risk during contract fulfillment.

Limited Market Visibility

Without direct access to local dynamics, companies rely on fragmented or delayed information.

As a result, pricing references, availability and operational constraints are often misunderstood or inaccurately assessed.

Execution Uncertainty

The absence of local coordination introduces uncertainty across the entire sourcing process.

Shipment execution, quality compliance and supplier performance become variables instead of controlled outcomes.
