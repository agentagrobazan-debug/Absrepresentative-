Context

Most sourcing challenges in Argentina are not caused by supply — but by lack of local control.

Why Companies Establish a Presence in Argentina

Lack of Origin Control

Companies sourcing from Argentina often operate remotely, without direct visibility over exporters or real execution conditions at origin.

This creates a structural gap between procurement decisions and actual market reality.

Supplier Risk Exposure

Purchasing decisions are frequently based on unverified information, leading to misaligned expectations and execution failures during contract fulfillment.

Limited Execution Visibility

Without direct access to local dynamics, companies rely on fragmented data. Pricing and operational constraints are often misunderstood or inaccurately assessed.

Transaction Uncertainty

The absence of local coordination makes shipment execution and quality compliance variables instead of controlled outcomes.
