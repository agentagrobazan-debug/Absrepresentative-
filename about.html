Foundation

Why ABS Exists

Built from market reality.

ABS was founded in Rosario, at the core of Argentina’s agri-industrial system, to address a structural imbalance in international trade. For decades, sourcing has been dominated by seller-side logic, where buyers operate remotely with limited visibility of real execution conditions at origin.

From information to execution control.

Built on direct experience in grain market operations and commercial validation, ABS bridges the gap between global procurement requirements and Argentine supply execution — transforming fragmented information into structured, executable sourcing.
