Control at Origin.

International sourcing in Argentina does not fail due to lack of supply — it fails due to lack of control at origin.

The Reality

Bridging the Execution Gap

Most procurement strategies in Argentina are built on indicative offers and remote promises. This creates an execution gap where commercial expectations rarely align with origin reality.

ABS was established to eliminate this uncertainty. We provide the technical presence required to validate, structure, and execute sourcing operations from within the Argentine market.

The ABS Mandate

To replace remote speculation with technical authority at origin. We ensure that what is contracted is actually executable.

Structure

Strict Buyer-Side Alignment

Conflict of interest is the primary cause of sourcing failure. To solve this, ABS is structurally unable to represent sellers.

We do not hold positions, we do not intermediate for profit from the spread, and we do not represent exporters. Our entire operational structure is legally and commercially locked to the buyer’s interest.

Approach

Technical Execution, Not Sales

Origin Authority

We operate from the core of the Argentine agri-industrial sector. Our visibility is direct, not filtered by third-party intermediaries.

Validated Sounding

We don't search for "offers"; we map executable conditions. Every supplier scenario is verified for volume, quality, and timeframe.

Sourcing Infrastructure

We provide the infrastructure for international companies to have their own commercial office in Argentina without the structural overhead.
