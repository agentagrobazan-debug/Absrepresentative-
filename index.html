Context

Bridging structural gaps in Argentina.

ABS was born from direct experience in the Argentine market and from years of observing a recurring pattern in international sourcing: genuine purchasing intent, yet decisions made from a distance — based on incomplete information, misaligned expectations and underestimated execution risk at origin.

International buyers need more than information.
They need independent judgment and structured control at origin.

ABS operates as the foreign buyer’s commercial office in Argentina, representing buyer interests exclusively and acting as a permanent strategic presence at origin.

Our role is not intermediation. It is responsibility, validation and disciplined execution.

Focused on structured and technically validated agri-industrial sourcing.

Selective Mandate Structure

Each mandate is accepted only when strategic alignment, technical feasibility and execution standards meet ABS structural criteria. We deliberately limit concurrent engagements to preserve execution integrity, strategic focus and full technical dedication for each client we represent.

Methodology

Buyer-Side Structure

A structured execution model designed for disciplined buyer-side control at origin.

01

Buyer Operating Context

Structured assessment of the buyer’s operational capacity and sourcing structure prior to formal engagement.

02

ABS Feasibility Intelligence Report

Structured technical report prepared by ABS, including supply validation, achievable quality parameters, executable volumes and execution risk mapping based on the buyer’s specific sourcing intent.

03

Local Representation

Execution control at origin: negotiation structuring, supplier DD, quality coordination, and operational tracking.

Framework

Engagement

1. Contractual Structure

The feasibility phase is governed by a formal service agreement defining scope, confidentiality and advisory responsibility.

If viability is confirmed, a separate representation agreement may be executed.

2. Authority & Mandate

Under representation, ABS operates under a specific power of attorney granted solely for the mandated transaction.

3. Selective Engagement Model

ABS maintains a limited number of active mandates to preserve execution integrity and strategic focus.

Engagements are formalized only when operational alignment and execution viability are structurally confirmed.

4. Compliance & Invoicing

ABS operates under Argentine tax registration and issues official electronic export invoices (Factura E) for international clients.
