Context

Bridging structural gaps in Argentina.

ABS was born from direct experience in the Argentine market and from years of observing a recurring pattern in international sourcing operations: genuine purchasing intent, yet decisions made from a distance — based on incomplete information, misaligned expectations and underestimated execution risk at origin.

That gap between intention and operational reality is what led to the creation of ABS. At ABS, we believe that international buyers need more than information: they need presence, technical judgment and real execution control at origin.

We operate as the foreign buyer’s commercial office in Argentina, representing buyer interests exclusively and serving as a permanent on-the-ground presence.

Our objective is to address the needs of foreign buyers in the Argentine market through technical analysis, structured validation and disciplined execution.

Focused on structured and technically validated agri-industrial sourcing.

Our role is not intermediation. It is responsibility, validation and structured execution.
